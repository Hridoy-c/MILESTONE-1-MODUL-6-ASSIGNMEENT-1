Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about the conference.

Who can attend the conference?

Anyone interested in software development is welcome, including students, professionals, freelancers, and tech enthusiasts.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes. All keynote sessions and selected workshops will be recorded and shared with registered attendees after the event.

Do I receive a certificate?

Yes. Every attendee will receive a digital certificate of participation after the conference.

Can I get a refund?

Refund requests are accepted up to 14 days before the event. After that period, tickets become non-refundable.

Is food included?

Lunch, coffee breaks, and refreshments are included with every ticket.

How do I contact the organizers?

You can reach us through our contact page or email our support team anytime before the event.
